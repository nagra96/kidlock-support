KidLock — Support KidLock locks your iPhone or iPad for toddlers using iOS Guided Access. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS Q: How do I unlock Kid Mode if I forget my PIN? A: Triple-click the side button on your iPhone → enter your iOS Guided Access passcode → tap End. To reset Guided Access passcode: Settings → Accessibility → Guided Access → Passcode Settings. Q: Does KidLock collect any data? A: No. KidLock collects no data and makes no network requests. Q: Does this work on iPhone and iPad?
